About Us

The team represents six students of Hochschule Rhein-Waal, Kamp-Lintfort, Germany currently studying Masters in Information Engineering and Computer Science. This project was done for the course “Applied Research Project A” in summer semester 2019. The project started in middle of April, 2019 and was finished before the end of June, 2019. The group comprises of were four members from Nepal and two from Bangladesh. The project was done under the supervision of Professor Dr, Timo Kahl and We would like to heartly thank the supervisor to guide us through the complexities of project and helping in completing the project. We are extremely grateful to him because without his proper guidance and support, it would have been extremely difficult to complete the project.

About Recommender System

Our recommender system recommends smartphone according to the need of the user. The user have to answer few questions and based on the answers our system recommends one major recommendation and four similar featured alternatives. Artificial Neural Networks has been used to train the system to recommend the best possible options.
